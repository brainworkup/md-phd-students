## Working Memory as RDoC Construct {background-color="#40666e"}

::: {.incremental}

- Limited capacity system
- Highly heritable trait
:::

## Project Innovation

1. Large-scale dataset (COGENT)
2. Novel analytical methods
3. Focus on causality
4. Pathway to treatments
5. Transdiagnostic implications
